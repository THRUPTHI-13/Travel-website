Places where you can visit

Panambur Beach

One of the most popular beaches near Mangalore, known for its cleanliness and events.

Kateel Temple

Famous temple dedicated to Goddess Durga Parameshwari, situated on the banks of river Nandini.

Malpe Beach (Udupi)

Golden sand beach famous for water sports and the boat ride to St. Mary’s Island.

St. Mary’s Island (Udupi)

Known for unique hexagonal basalt rock formations and crystal clear water.

Kaup Lighthouse (Udupi)

A serene beach with a historic lighthouse offering panoramic sea views.
